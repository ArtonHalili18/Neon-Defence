Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

Neon Defense ("the App") is a single-player tower defense game. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what data the App collects and how it is used.

Data Collection

The App does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All game progress (high scores, settings) is stored locally on your device and never leaves it.

No Analytics or Tracking

The App contains no third-party analytics, tracking SDKs, advertising frameworks, or telemetry of any kind.

No Accounts

The App does not require or support user accounts, logins, or registrations.

No In-App Purchases

The App contains no in-app purchases, subscriptions, or microtransactions.

No Advertising

The App displays no advertisements.

Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. Since no data is collected from any user, the App is safe for all ages.

Data Storage

Game settings and progress are saved locally on the device using standard browser storage. This data can be cleared at any time through the App's settings or by deleting the App.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date above.
